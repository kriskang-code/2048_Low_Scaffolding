How to Play

Goal

Merge tiles to create the 2048 tile. After winning, keep playing for a higher score.

Controls

Arrow Keys or W A S D Move tiles
Swipe Move tiles (mobile)
X Toggle swap mode
B Toggle remove mode

Rules

  1. Slide tiles in any direction.
  2. Matching tiles merge into one with double the value.
  3. A new tile (2 or 4) spawns after every move.
  4. Score increases by the merged tile's value.

Power-Ups (2 uses each per game)

U
Undo

Revert your last move.

S
Swap

Exchange any two tiles. Tap first, then second.

R
Remove

Destroy any tile from the board.

Game Over

The game ends when the board is full and no merges are possible.

Tips

  • Build your highest tile in a corner.
  • Save power-ups for tight situations.
  • Work in one primary direction.
